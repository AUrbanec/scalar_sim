Simulation Overview
About the Simulation

This simulation models scalar (longitudinal) electromagnetic waves within a resonant structure. It implements a finite-difference time-domain (FDTD) scheme to solve modified Maxwell equations in terms of the scalar potential (Ï•) and vector potential (A).

The primary goal is to study the conditions under which a predominantly scalar (longitudinal) mode can be excited in a resonant cavity or parallel plate configuration.
